# Project Details
Project Title: Residential Management System

## Problem Statement:
Manually managing apartment complexes is commonly known to lead to many problems, such as miscommunication between the administrators and residents, uncompleted maintenance requests and improper payment schedule. As a result, these issues born from miscommunication make for a waste of time, confusion and unsatisfactory productivity for everyone included, be they residents, administrators or handymen staff.

## Proposed Solution:
Our proposed solution is a Residential Management System that functions as a bridge between the apartment complex administrators, the residents and facility/service staff (optional), all within one platform. Through this platform, the residents will be able to view their impending payments, such as apartment or parking rent as well as maintenance fees, with the due date visible alongside the payment status. On the other hand, administrators can check and confirm payment status for each resident. The residents can also submit maintenance requests or book handymen services based on their need through the list provided on the platform, with the option for the handymen to reject the request based on their schedule (Optional). This means that the administrator will no longer need to act as a connecting bridge for petty grievances on the daily, since the residents have all the information they need on the platform. Additionally, since the system will be able to provide real-time communication, the administrator can make announcements, keeping the users notified easily of important updates. Overall, this platform aims to streamline managing of apartment complexes by making communication easier, simplifying payment procedure and improving booking of services, for a more organized and efficient experience overall.


## Project Scope:
- Aim: To streamline managing of apartment complexes by organizing communication and tracking into one platform. 

- Objectives:
1.	Provide a user-friendly dashboard for each role (admin, resident, handyman)
2.	Allow residents to view rent, parking & maintenance fees with due dates and admins to confirm payments
3.	Enable residents to submit and track maintenance requests efficiently & book handymen directly with acceptance by the handyman (optional)
4.	Enable handymen to manage tasks, update statuses and track schedules via a calendar
5.	Allow administrators to manage multiple buildings/apartments using unique IDs
6.	Enable administrators to post announcements 
7.	Allow residents to view announcements for updates 
8.	Ensure role-based access and security

## Application Description:
The Residential Management System consists of  three main users: Administrator, Resident and Handyman. 
-Administrators are able to manage the apartments and residents of different complexes, track payment developments for each tenant, post about necessary announcements, upload and edit handyman information. 
-Residents are able to view their upcoming payment details and status, get notified about new updates, book handymen based on need for their services.
-Handymen are able to view their booking requests, accept or reject it, then give status updates as pending or completed, manage availability and view their calendar.
