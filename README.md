# Project Details
Project Title: Residential Management System

## Problem Statement:
Manually managing apartment complexes is commonly known to lead to many problems, such as miscommunication between the administrators and residents and improper payment schedule.  As a result, these issues born from miscommunication make for a waste of time, confusion and unsatisfactory productivity for everyone included, be they residents or administrators.

## Proposed Solution:
Our proposed solution is a Residential Management System that functions as a bridge between the apartment complex administrators and the residents, all within one platform. Through this platform, the residents will be able to view their impending payments, such as apartment, parking rent, or maintenance fees, with the due date visible alongside the payment status, as well as view handyman's contact information. On the other hand, administrators can check and confirm payment status for each resident. Additionally, since the system will be able to provide real-time communication, the administrator can make announcements, keeping the users notified easily of important updates. Overall, this platform aims to streamline the management of apartment complexes by improving communication and providing clear visibility of invoices and due dates, resulting in a more organized and efficient experience for all users.

## Project Scope:
- Aim: To streamline managing of apartment complexes by organizing communication and tracking into one platform. 

- Objectives:
1.	Provide a user-friendly dashboard for each role (admin, resident)
2.	Allow residents to view rent, parking & maintenance fees with due dates and admins to confirm payments
3.	Allow administrators to manage multiple buildings/apartments using unique IDs
4.	Enable administrators to post announcements 
5.	Allow residents to view announcements for updates 
6.	Ensure role-based access and security

## Application Description:
The Residential Management System consists of two main users: Administrator & Resident. 
-Administrators are able to manage the apartments and residents of different complexes, track payment developments for each tenant, post about necessary announcements. 
-Residents are able to view their upcoming payment details and status and get notified about new updates, as well as view handyman's contact information.
